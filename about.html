About Bruno's Aquariums

Local, family-run shop for saltwater & freshwater tanks, fish, corals, gear, and expert maintenance.

Our Story

We started Bruno’s Aquariums to help hobbyists build beautiful, healthy aquariums — whether it’s your first freshwater tank or a custom reef showpiece. We focus on honest advice, quarantined livestock, and reliable service so your system thrives for the long haul.

From tank selection and aquascaping to water chemistry and long-term care, we love guiding customers at every step.

What We Offer

  • Aquariums & custom builds (freshwater & reef)
  • Accessories & parts: filtration, pumps, lighting, plumbing, rock/substrate, foods
  • Quarantined freshwater & marine fish, corals, and invertebrates
  • On-site maintenance & emergency support

Our Commitment

We believe aquariums bring peace, beauty, and a sense of wonder into homes and businesses. Our goal is to make that experience stress-free with healthy livestock, dependable products, and hands-on guidance.
